## Repeated Execution

The last statement of the function is generally the function call to continue to the next step.

Often times we use parameters to act as the stored data, similar to a index in a loop.

Look at double_array.py for an example of how to pass variables.

## Subproblems

Recursive functions can often be though of as lots of little subproblems.

Take factorial for example...

factorial(6) can be though of as 6 * factorial(5)

In this example factorial(5) is a subproblem of factorial(6)
